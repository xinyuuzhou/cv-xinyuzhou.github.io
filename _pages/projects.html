Global, regional, and national trends in Covid-19 vaccination intention and confidence on social media
DATASET: Covid-19 vaccine tweets dataset
Paper: [Manuscript submitted] [Appendix 1]
- As of this writing, this is the largest global study comparing Covid-19 vaccination intention and confidence, which provides insights into several countries that were not part of previous surveys.
- Moreover, this is the first study to assess trends in Covid-19 vaccination confidence and intention at global, regional, and national levels. This is also the first study to track these trends at the state level in the USA.
- In addition, this study is the first to conduct a global assessment and comparison of distrust in policymaking and misinformation related to Covid-19 vaccination.
- In this study, over 6.7 million English-language tweets were collected and more than 8000 tweets were labelled manually. To analyze the collected tweets, CT-BERT was fine-tuned to implement a deep learning model.
- I co-conceptualized this study as well as fine-tuned the CT-BERT model used in this study. At the same time, I also worked on data collection and analysis, designed the statistical analysis, and co-drafted the first versions of the manuscript and appendix.
Public response on Weibo during Covid-19 pandemic in China.
DATASET: COVID-19 Weibo Dataset
Paper: [Journal article] [Manuscript submitted]
- This study evaluated social media data to assess public responses to the Covid-19 pandemic in China. At the same time, public responses on social media during the resurgence of the Covid-19 pandemic in June 2020 in Beijing were also analyzed.
- Over 10,000,000 Weibo posts were collected and analyzed by using Linguistic Inquiry and Word Count text analysis programs, as well as Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA).
- I wrote several Python scripts for this project. I also co-wrote a (journal article and a submitted manuscript) on this project.
A comparison of public attitude towards Covid-19 test in China and in the UK
Paper: [Manuscript submitted]
- Using social media data, this study compared and evaluated public responses to Covid-19 testing in China and in the UK.
- 3856 tweets mentioning Covid-19 testing in the UK as well as 9299 Weibo posts in China were manually coded. The data was analyzed by using time trend, geographical mapping, and chi-squared test.
- I wrote a number of Python scripts used in this project.
Cross-country comparison of public awareness, rumors, and behavioral responses to the COVID-19 epidemic
Paper: [Journal article]
- This study compared global buying and online searching behaviors during the Covid-19 pandemic, particularly those that were related to products such as face masks, traditional medicines, disinfectants, hand sanitizers, thermometers, and antimalarial drugs.
- Data for the analysis were collected from Ali Index, Baidu Index, and Google Trends.
