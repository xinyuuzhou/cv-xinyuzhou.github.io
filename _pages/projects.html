Global, regional, and national trends in Covid-19 vaccination intention and confidence on social media
DATASET: Covid-19 vaccine tweets dataset
Paper: [Manuscript submitted] [Appendix 1]
- To our knowledge, this is by far the largest global comparison study of Covid-19 vaccination intention and confidence, providing an insight for many countries not yet covered in previous surveys.
- This is also the first study which assess trends in Covid-19 vaccination intention and confidence at global, regional, and national level.
- Additionally, this study is the first to globally assess and compare distrust in policymaker and misinformation regarding Covid-19 vaccination.
- In this study, we collected over 6.7 million English-language tweets, mannually labelled more than 8000 tweets, and trained a deep learning model to analyze tweet by finetuning CT-BERT.
- I co-conceptualized this study, finetuned the CT-BERT model, collected and analyzed the data, designed statistical analysis, and co-drafted the first version of the manuscript and the appendix.
Public response on Weibo during Covid-19 pandemic in China.
DATASET: COVID-19 Weibo Dataset
Paper: [Journal article] [Manuscript submitted]
- Our study assessed public's response to Covid-19 pandemic in China with social media data. We also compared it with public's response during the resurgence of Covid-19 during June 2020 in Beijing on social media.
- We collected over 10,000,000 Weibo posts and analyzed them with Linguistic Inquiry and Word Count text analysis program and latent Dirichlet allocation.
- I write a lot of Python scripts and parts of the manuscripts (a journal article and a submitted manuscript).
A comparison of public attitude towards Covid-19 test in China and in the UK
Paper: [Manuscript submitted]
- With social media data, this study compares public's response to Covid-19 test in China and in the UK
- We mannually coded 3856 tweets in the UK and 9299 Weibo posts in China mentioning Covid-19 test. Time trend, geographical mapping, and chi-squared test are applied.
- I write a number of Python scripts involved in this project.
Cross-country comparison of public awareness, rumors, and behavioral responses to the COVID-19 epidemic
Paper: [Journal article]
- This study globally compared buying/searching behavior towards mask/traditional medicine/disinfectant/hand sanitizer/thermometer/antimalarial drugs during Covid-19 pandemic.
- We collected data from Google Trends, Baidu Index, and Ali Index
